=== AMBITION ===

Ambition is a Simple, Clean and Flat Responsive WordPress Theme. It is easily customizable with customize. Some of the highlighted options are two site layout (Narrow and Wide), 4 layouts for every post/page, 2 Page Templates, 8 Widget Areas, 7 Custom Widgets, Featured slider to highlight your post, Social Profile Navigation and many more. Also has custom background, header, menu, favicon, CSS, webclip icon, and logo. Supports popular plugins like WPML, Breadcrumb NavXT, WP-PageNavi and Contact Form 7. It is also translation ready. Get free support at http://themehorse.com/support-forum/ and View beautiful demo site at http://themehorse.com/preview/ambition
